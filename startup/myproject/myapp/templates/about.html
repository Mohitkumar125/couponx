Reward customers instantly, boost your sales, and simplify prize management with our all-in-one digital lucky draw platform.

What is the CouponX?

The CouponX is a smart, fully online solution for shop owners to reward customers instantly after purchases. It removes the need for physical raffle tickets and complicated manual tracking. Shop owners can easily generate unique coupon codes with expiry dates, share them via QR codes or direct links, and customers can instantly check and claim their prizes online.
